Welcome!

Thank you for your participation. This is a study about language comprehension (in English) conducted by the University of Oslo and the University of Paris Cite.

The study will ask you to carry out two tasks simultaneously. While you store two letters in your memory you will be asked to determine the extent to which you agree with a sentence that describes a picture.

It is important that you complete the task without interruption and in a quiet environment. Please turn off your computer's speakers throughout the experiment.

Your task will be explained in greater detail in the following pages.

Your participation in this study will remain confidential. Your individual privacy will be maintained in all published and written data resulting from the study. You may print this form for your records.
